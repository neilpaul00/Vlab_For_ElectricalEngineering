PROCEDURE:

  1. Click on the power switch to turn it on.
  2. In the control panel, click the checkboxes to select PT and CT ratios.
    3. Please proceed further according to additional instructions:
  3. Vary the slider for "True value of voltage".
  4. Vary the slider for "True value of current".
  5. Observe the Wattmeter Reading.
  6. Click on the "Add to Table" button to insert your observations and note the error.
  7. Take 5-10 readings.
